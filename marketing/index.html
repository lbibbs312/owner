The clipboard tax

If parts move between your plants, you're running blind.

Your shuttle drivers, your trailers, your racks — and no single place that shows what's moving, what's short, and what just failed inspection. Here's what that costs you.

01

Counts don't match

48 pieces leave staging, 28 show up at unrack. Nobody notices until the line goes down.

02

Damage you can't prove

A rack comes back bent. Was it you or the leasing company? Without a time-stamped record, you eat the chargeback.

03

Paper inspections

DVIRs live in a binder in the cab. When the DOT audit comes, good luck.

04

Racks vanish

Returnable racks and dunnage drift between sites with no chain of custody. You re-buy what you already own.
